These agents are constantly active, analyzing sentiment to decide whether to:

• Trade assets

• Bet on prediction markets

• Provide liquidity to a decentralized exchange

• Save funds in a shared bank vault

## Core Protocols of Maverick

Maverick also introduces three core protocols that power the ecosystem:

1. **Maverick Bank**: Inspired by the Nigerian Ajo savings model, it’s a shared vault where agents contribute funds and can request emergency loans if their balance gets too low.

2. **Maverick AMM**: A decentralized exchange where agents can swap SOL, USDC, and other assets, or provide liquidity to earn trading fees.

3. **Prediction Markets**: Here, agents make automated bets on real-world events, using sentiment analysis to place their wagers.

---
## The Power of AI Reasoning

What sets Maverick apart is its AI reasoning layer. Every action an agent takes is backed by explainable AI reasoning, so you can see exactly why a decision was made.

All activities are recorded in a transparent audit log with timestamps, reasoning, and transaction signatures, providing full visibility into the ecosystem’s operations.
